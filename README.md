##  Project Overview

This project is based on the role of a *Student Digital Ambassador*, where the objective is to help peers understand and navigate the digital world effectively. The project focuses on building essential digital literacy skills such as creating a professional online presence, practicing safe internet usage, understanding digital communication, and being aware of cyber threats.

The project consists of five modules, each designed to develop a specific aspect of digital literacy. All tasks are organized within this GitHub repository, along with supporting files, screenshots, and a detailed project report. Through this project, I gained practical experience using various online platforms and improved my understanding of responsible digital behavior.

---

##  Module Summaries

###  Module 1: Digital Literacy Infographic
In this module, I created a one-page infographic to explain the concept of digital literacy and its importance. The design includes key topics such as safe internet practices, smart digital tools and professional online presence. This task helped me understand how to present information in a clear and visually appealing way.

---

###  Module 2: Digital Portfolio
In this module, I built my professional online presence using platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. I created and updated my profiles, added relevant information, and understood the importance of maintaining a digital portfolio. This will help me showcase my skills and achievements in the future.

---

###  Module 3: Coding & Collaboration Platforms
In this module, I explored platforms like HackerRank and Google Forms. I solved a beginner-level coding problem to improve my programming skills and created a digital literacy quiz using Google Forms. This helped me understand both coding practice and online collaboration tools.

---

###  Module 4: Email Etiquette & Communication
In this module, I learned how to communicate professionally in the digital world. I drafted formal emails for academic and professional purposes and created a checklist of social media do’s and don’ts. This task improved my understanding of proper communication etiquette.

---

###  Module 5: Cybercrime Awareness
In this module, I studied a cybercrime case and created a prevention checklist. I learned about different types of online threats and how to stay safe while using digital platforms. This helped me become more aware of cybersecurity practices.
