## Description
This infographic highlights essential aspects of digital awareness and productivity for students and online users. It emphasizes safe internet practices such as using strong passwords, enabling two-factor authentication, and avoiding suspicious links. The content also showcases useful digital tools like Notion, Evernote, and Microsoft OneNote for organizing academic work efficiently. It promotes collaborative platforms like Google Workspace for seamless teamwork. Additionally, it introduces study tools like Quizlet and Anki that enhance learning through active recall and spaced repetition. The infographic concludes by defining digital literacy as the ability to effectively find, evaluate, create, and communicate information using digital technologies.
