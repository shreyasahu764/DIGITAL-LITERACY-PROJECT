## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank or trusted service  
- Do not approve unknown or unexpected payment requests on UPI apps  
- Always verify the identity of callers or messages before taking any action  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links received through SMS, email, or social media  
- Use only official and trusted apps for financial transactions and downloads  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for added account security  
- Regularly check your bank account and transaction history for any unusual activity  
- Install apps only from official app stores and avoid third-party sources  

### Financial & UPI Safety Tips
- Remember that you do NOT need to enter your UPI PIN to receive money  
- Always double-check payment details before sending money to anyone  
- Do not scan unknown QR codes, as they may lead to fraudulent transactions  

### Reporting Cybercrime in India
- Report incidents at: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Call Cybercrime Helpline: **1930**
